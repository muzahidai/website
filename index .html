Short Bio

A. A. M. MUZAHID is currently a lecturer of the School of Electronic and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai University of Engineering Science (SUES), China. He received Ph.D. in Information and Communication system with expertise in 3D computer vision. He is an active researcher with a record of scholarly publications. He has around 2 years of working experience in teaching at University. He received two distinguished awards and several appreciation certificates during his doctoral study.

Research Interest

His research interests include Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition, 3D vision, particularly on 3D feature learning, 3D object recognition, 3D modeling, scene understanding, and AR and VR.
